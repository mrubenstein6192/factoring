Ready to Learn Factoring?!


The most important part of factoring is NOT being afraid to be wrong and try again!
It's a bit of a "guess-and-check!"
Standard form for a quadratic expression is
ax2 + bx + c
Your goal is to find two numbers that multiply to "c" and add up to "b".
You will also have to decide between " + " and " - " for each number.
